About Us

Our church is a community of faith, dedicated to serving and supporting one another.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: The mission of West Bauxite MBC is to Preach the Word and Reach the World for Christ.

Vision: It is our vision to impact the people of Bauxite, AR with the message and power of the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

What We Believe

West Bauxite Missionary Baptist Church is doctrinally aligned with the American Baptist Association.
abaptist.org | Doctrinal Statement

Supported Missions

We believe in spreading God's love through missions both locally and globally.

Worldwide Literature Project

A project that aims to provide Christian literature and materials to people worldwide.

Budd Creek Missionary Baptist Camp

A summer camp dedicated to youth outreach and ministry in a Christian environment.

Calvary MBC - Anthony Phillips

Supporting the ministry and outreach efforts of Calvary Missionary Baptist Church under Pastor Anthony Phillips.

Daniel Estrada - Spain

Supporting the missionary work of Daniel Estrada in Spain, spreading the gospel to new communities.

Arkansas State Mission Project

Statewide efforts to strengthen and grow local churches and ministries across Arkansas.

Interstate Foreign Missions (ABA)

Supporting the American Baptist Association's efforts in reaching foreign nations with the gospel message.

About Gary Self

Brother Gary is a retired veterinarian and didn’t answer God’s call to the ministry until he was 38. Since then, he has pastored churches in Arkansas and Mississippi. He has Bachelor of Theology and Master of Bible Languages degrees. Brother Gary loves to explain God’s Word in an understandable manner, but also in a way that challenges people to think about how their lives measure up to the principles of God’s Word. He began his role as pastor of West Bauxite MBC January 2023. He is married to Dorothy and has one son living and 8 grandchildren.

“How then shall they call on him in whom they have not believed? and how shall they believe in him of whom they have not heard? and how shall they hear without a preacher?” -Romans 10:14

About Toby Wilson

Toby serves as both Associate and Youth Pastor of West Bauxite. As Youth Pastor, he provides spiritual guidance to the youth. He is a faithful teacher of the teens and makes their Biblical education a priority as he leads them in their spiritual walks. He holds a Bachelor of Science and Education Degree from Henderson State University. He married his wife Bridget in 2001 and together they have two children, Isaac and Bella. He came to West Bauxite as a Youth Director in 2005 and was later ordained as Assocaite/Youth Pastor in 2019. He also holds a Master of Divinity from Central Arkansas Baptist Bible Institute (CABBI) where he serves as an instructor.

“But speaking the truth in love, may grow up into him all things, which is the head, even Christ:” -Ephesians 4:15

Kristy Hall

The Bible has a lot to say about music, and Kristy’s role is to lead the church’s worship of God in the singing of songs, praises, and hymns. Kristy also directs our Adult and Children’s Choir and encourages each member to use their talents. She has been a faithful music leader at WB since 2016 and an encouragement to our church since before then. Kristy holds a degree in Music Education.

“Speaking to yourselves in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, singing and making melody in your hearts to the Lord:” -Ephesians 5:19
